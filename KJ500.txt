\item $\displaystyle \lim_{x\rightarrow-2} \dfrac{3x^3+2x^2-19x-22}{x^2+8x+12} = \lim_{x\rightarrow-2} \dfrac{(x+2)(3x^2-4x-11)}{(x+2)(x+6)} = \lim_{x\rightarrow-2} \dfrac{(3x^2-4x-11)}{(x+6)} = \dfrac{9}{4}$
